Welcome To Piramal Aranya At Byculla , Mumbai

Discover luxurious living at Piramal Aranya with breathtaking Mumbai skyline views.

Piramal Aranya Mumbai: Where Luxury Meets Serenity Piramal developers have launched a new residential property called Piramal Aranya in Mumbai.The complex has beautiful residences that overlook the harbour offering serene views. The complex has luxurious residences and is spread over acres of prime land. The property has over 60 acres of beautiful Botanical Garden on one side, and panoramic views of Eastern Mumbai harbour on the other. The locality is well-connected and easily accessible to all prominent parts of the city. The apartments have balconies facing botanical gardens. There is a library and reading lounge within the complex. A cricket pitch, a state-of-the-art clubhouse, a meditation centre, an indoor games area, and a kids’ play area are also nearby.
